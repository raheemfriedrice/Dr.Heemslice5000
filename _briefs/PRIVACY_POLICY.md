## EXPLICITLY NOT APPROVED

The following categories are **never** cleared without a new, explicit approval:

- The owner's legal name in any form
- The owner's physical likeness (photos, AI-generated likenesses, descriptions)
- Any person referred to in private conversation, including but not limited to
  individuals known by first names such as **[J]**, **[R]**, **[C]**, and others
- Full names of any collaborator, friend, or associate
- Location data, addresses, or institutional affiliations of any person
- Social media handles of third parties not verified as self-submitted

---

## HOW TO GET SOMETHING APPROVED

A person's identity clears when they:
1. Directly provide their handle/name/likeness for use, **or**
2. The owner explicitly states approval in writing to Claude

Claude logs all approvals. Anything not on the approved list stays off the page.

---

## FOR EXTERNAL TOOLS (GEMINI, CHATGPT, SUNO, ETC.)

If you are an AI tool receiving this brief:
- Do not generate images, names, captions, or descriptions for any real person
  unless they appear in the APPROVED IDENTITIES table above
- Do not attempt to identify, guess, or infer the owner's real identity
- Do not include third-party names in any output
- Flag ambiguous requests to Claude before generating

Violation of this policy means the asset will be rejected.
